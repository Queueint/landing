Your time - Our responsibility

Qint provides queue management system efficiency and without hardware and maintenance costs while advertising to customers, analyzing data and preventing queue abandonment.

*Free of charge, no credit card required

How does it work?

Scan

Upon arrival the customer will scan a QR code / send SMS to the phone number appearing on a sign.

Receive

The customer will receive a number in the queue and see the state of the queue, estimated waiting time, and will be able to set a reminder shortly before their turn.

Continue

Then the customer will be free to continue doing his business, while keeping track of his turn, and watching advertisments.
