Smart queue management system

Qint lets organizations procure queue management in minutes with comfort, without hardware and maintenance costs while advertising to your customers, analyzing data and preventing queue abandonment.

Free of charge, no credit card required

How does it work?

Scan

Upon arrival the customer will scan a Qr code / send SMS to the phone number on a sign and will receive a number in the queue. (It is possible to enqueue remotely)

Receive

The customer will receive a number in the queue and see how many customers wait before them, estimated waiting time, and will be able to set a reminder shortly before their turn.

Continue

The customer will choose if to continue their shopping spree, eat something in the meantime or view advertisements from the business and its surroundings, while preserving their place in the queue.
